The challenge

What actually breaks in bars & pubs.

01

Open tabs across many staff

Any bartender can add to any tab. Servers transfer tabs from bar to table with one tap. Running totals always accurate.

02

Happy hour without manager overrides

Schedule price drops by hour and weekday. 17–19 is automatically discounted, no manual key-in, no errors.

03

Split bills, evenly or per item

Six guests, one tab — split evenly, or have each pay for what they ordered. Card splits, cash splits, tip-on-split all handled.

04

Tip pool & ID verification

Automatic tip pool calculation by shift. ID verification prompts on age-restricted items. Audit trail for every late-night override.
